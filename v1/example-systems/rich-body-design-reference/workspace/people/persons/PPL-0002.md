# Malik Chen

## BIO

Malik maintains execution runbooks, captures operational evidence during incidents, and turns ad hoc mitigations into repeatable procedures.

### Focus Areas

- Trace capture during degraded tool execution
- Turning mitigation steps into stable runbooks
- Translating research findings into operator-facing checklists

## WORKSTYLE

### Communication Defaults

- Writes while others speak so the incident record stays current.
- Favors concrete examples over abstract policy wording when documenting a fix.

### Review Habits

1. Dry-runs commands before adding them to a runbook.
2. Adds stop conditions next to each destructive step.
3. Reviews edge cases after the immediate incident is stable.

## ESCALATION_PREFERENCES

- Use chat for routine documentation questions.
- Page when a runbook step is ambiguous during live recovery.
- Include the exact command, observed output, and what the operator expected instead.
