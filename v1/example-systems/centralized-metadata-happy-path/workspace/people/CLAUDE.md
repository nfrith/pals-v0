# people/

The directory of everyone who matters in this system. If someone owns a backlog item, runs an experiment, or collaborates on anything — they have a record here.

## Philosophy

People records are stable. A person's profile describes who they are and what role they play, not what they're working on today. Transient task updates belong in backlog items, not here.

This module has no dependencies — it depends on nothing. But almost everything else depends on it. Breaking a person record breaks refs across the entire system.

## Key Directories

- `persons/` — Individual person files as `PPL-NNNNNN.md`
